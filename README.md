## üõ† Using This Project

The easiest way to get this project up and running:
- Download Unity Hub over at [Unity](https://unity.com/download).
- In Unity Hub at the top right press ADD > ADD PROJECT FROM DISK.
- You might get a notification that the Editor version is not installed. I recommend installing the exact editor version as otherwise issues might arise.
- Open the project, initial import might take a while.
- Open Assets > MAIN > Scenes > Level_0 or Level_1 (Other scenes are unfinished, best to use this as a base for custom levels).
- Experiment / Or copy the scene and make a custom level!

## üéÆ Controls

- Turn: R stick. Level_0 shows an example for a menu to change turn type and turn settings.
- Skate: Hold L/R grip and swing your arm.
- Jump: Hold Both L+R grip and swing your arm.
- Air Dash: Hold Both L+R grip and swing your arm while in the air.
- Brake: Hold both L+R grip for a second to slow down
- Overclock Boost: Press Forward on the L stick to overclock when you have at least 1 battery segment filled.
- Grab: Press and hold L/R grip when the grab indicator appears (only in level_1)
- Phone: L stick click to open and close, L stick to navigate, Y to select an app.
